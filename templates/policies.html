🇮🇳 Government of India

Indian Farming Policies & Schemes

Comprehensive guide to government schemes, subsidies and support available for Indian farmers

💰
Income Support

PM-KISAN

प्रधानमंत्री किसान सम्मान निधि

Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers across India, paid in 3 installments of ₹2,000 each.

Benefit ₹6,000/year in 3 installments
Eligibility Small & marginal farmers with cultivable land
Apply pmkisan.gov.in or nearest CSC center
✅ ₹6,000 Annual Benefit
🛡️
Insurance

PMFBY

प्रधानमंत्री फसल बीमा योजना

Comprehensive crop insurance covering losses from natural calamities, pests and diseases at very low premium rates for farmers.

Premium 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi crops
Coverage Natural disasters, pests, diseases, drought
Apply pmfby.gov.in or nearest bank branch
✅ Low Premium Insurance
💧
Irrigation

PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana

प्रधानमंत्री कृषि सिंचाई योजना

Ensures water access to every farm field. Promotes micro-irrigation like drip and sprinkler systems with subsidies up to 55%.

Subsidy Up to 55% for small farmers on drip/sprinkler
Goal Har Khet Ko Pani — water to every farm
Apply State agriculture department office
✅ Up to 55% Subsidy
🌱
Organic Farming

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

परम्परागत कृषि विकास योजना

Promotes organic farming practices with financial assistance to farmers transitioning from conventional to organic methods.

Benefit ₹50,000/hectare over 3 years
Eligibility Farmer groups of minimum 50 farmers
Apply State agriculture department
✅ ₹50,000/hectare
🏦
Credit

Kisan Credit Card

किसान क्रेडिट कार्ड

Provides farmers with affordable credit for agricultural needs including seeds, fertilizers, pesticides and farm equipment.

Interest 4% per annum with interest subvention
Credit Limit Based on land holding and crop type
Apply Any nationalized bank or cooperative bank
✅ 4% Interest Rate
🏪
Market

eNAM

राष्ट्रीय कृषि बाजार

National Agriculture Market — online trading platform connecting farmers directly to buyers across India for better prices.

Benefit Better price discovery, direct market access
Coverage 1000+ mandis across 18 states
Register enam.gov.in or nearest APMC mandi
✅ Better Market Prices
💻
Technology

Digital Agriculture Mission

डिजिटल कृषि मिशन

Promotes use of AI, drones, remote sensing and digital tools in agriculture to improve productivity and reduce input costs.

Focus AI, drones, IoT sensors for smart farming
Benefit Subsidized drones for crop spraying
Info agricoop.nic.in
✅ Drone Subsidies
🧪
Soil Health

Soil Health Card Scheme

मृदा स्वास्थ्य कार्ड योजना

Provides farmers with soil health cards showing nutrient status of their soil with recommendations for appropriate dosage of nutrients.

Benefit Free soil testing every 2 years
Includes 12 parameters tested including NPK, pH
Apply soilhealth.dac.gov.in
✅ Free Soil Testing
🍎
Horticulture

MIDH

एकीकृत बागवानी विकास मिशन

Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture — supports cultivation of fruits, vegetables, flowers, spices and plantation crops.

Subsidy 40-100% on planting material, tools
Covers Fruits, vegetables, flowers, spices
Apply State horticulture department
✅ Up to 100% Subsidy
